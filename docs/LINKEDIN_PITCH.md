## 🎬 The Pitch (verbatim)

> If you run legal or operations at a mid-sized company, you already know the pain.
>
> Your team reviews hundreds of contracts a year. Every NDA. Every MSA. Every vendor agreement. And every one of them is read by a senior lawyer who's billing you three to eight hundred dollars, taking two to five business days, and applying rules that are sometimes different from the last reviewer.
>
> That's the problem we built **ClauseLens** to solve.
>
> ClauseLens is an AI-powered contract review platform. You upload a PDF or Word document, and within two minutes you get the contract segmented into clauses, every clause scored against your firm's playbook, every risk flagged with a citation to the specific rule it violated, and a suggested redline you can accept, reject, or edit.
>
> Built for legal teams and ops teams who handle 25 to 100 contracts a month. Multi-tenant, SOC 2-aligned, GDPR-ready, append-only audit log on every action. Three roles — Admin, Contract Owner, Reviewer. Self-service signup, your data isolated, your playbook yours.
>
> The result? The same review that took a week takes a meeting. The senior lawyer reviews the exceptions, not the boilerplate. Compliance gets the paper trail. The business gets the deal.
>
> **ClauseLens. Your firm's first-line contract reviewer. Always on. Always consistent. Always cited.**
>
